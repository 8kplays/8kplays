30+ Cricket Quotes to Motivate You

8kplays | Mon 26 October 2020

When it’s time to name the most excited sports then cricket is one of them. Because in cricket one ball can decide which team is going to win. We can’t guess what happen on the second ball. When you are playing in the field with thousands of people crowd around you cheering up, then you have to stay motivate you and keep your focus in the match. Below you can find some cricket quotes to motivate you when match is not on your side. To motivate and inspire you to give your best that you can.

quotes for cricket

Cricket as a passion is distinctly contagious.

Cricket to us was more than play, It was a worship in the summer sun. – Edmund Blunden

Actually nobody can be perfect in cricket. Everybody makes mistakes. It is important to learn from your mistakes and correct them. – Kumar Sangakkara

My job is to perform, enjoy cricket and thank God for whatever he has given me. – Suresh Raina

Cricket is battle and service and sport and art. – Douglas Jardine

The true test of any cricketer’s character is how he reacts when the going gets tough, when it’s time to dig in. – Michael Holding

cricket love quotes

Every morning I woke up, I believed that I could score runs for India. – Sourav Ganguly

Cricket is the most important thing to me, so rest pales in comparison. – Virat Kohli

The joy of doing well as a batsman for your country is much more than that little joy of going for a party and enjoying music. It is a completely different high, and I get high by performances. That’s what I enjoy now. – Virat Kohli

Enjoy the game & Chase your dreams. Dreams do come true. – Sachin Tendulkar

My first bat was shaped of a coconut branch and from that day, all I wanted to do was to be a cricketer. – Brian Lara

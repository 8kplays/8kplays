Famous Archery Quotes You Want To Know

8kplays | Thu 07 January 2021

Archery is not just a bow and arrow. It is much more than that. It's as old as mankind. It teaches us to be the focus and patient in our life. Below you can find some famous archery quotes to inspire you.

Archery Inspirational Quotes

The history of the bow and arrow is the history of mankind. - Fred Bear

Archery does not get difficult or hard to understand until the arrow misses. - Milan E. Elott

A good archer is known not by his arrows but by his aim. - Thomas Fuller

The worst thing is to try to shoot as well as your previous shot. - Unknown

Archery is a feeling you can’t explain. - Unknown

It’s not about being the best. It’s about being better than you were yesterday. - Unknown

But you must be patient and careful; nor should you expect to become an accomplished archer without long and severe training. - Maurice Thompson

The archer that shoots badly has a lie ready. - Spanish Proverb

Nothing clears a troubled mind better than shooting a bow. - Fred Bear

You have to relax when you’re shooting an arrow. You can’t be tense. And that just helps, in your day-to-day life. - Stephen Amell

Whether you are a beginning, intermediate, or even advanced archer, Archery will give you something that’s almost impossible to find elsewhere. - Usa Archery

Other archers are not my competition. I stand with them, not against them. - Unknown

An arrow is never afraid of shooting from the bow; but it is afraid of not reaching the target! - Mehmet Murat ildan

Even as the archer loves the arrow that flies, so too he loves the bow that remains constant in his hands. - Nigerian Proverb

I shoot an arrow into the air, where it lands I do not care: I get my arrows wholesale! - Curly Howard

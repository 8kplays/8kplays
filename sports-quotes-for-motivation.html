Most Powerful Sports Quotes for Motivation

Deepak | Mon 23 November 2020

Sports are the most important part of our life. We all play many sports in our life. Some are for fun or others find their life in it. While doing that sometimes we need to get the motivation to play more or to overcome our failure. For those time sports quotes for motivation make a lot of help. With them, we get motivation and learn that failure is just part of success. Sometimes we just need to do hard work and never ever quit trying.

Sports Quotes for Motivation

There may be people that have more talent than you, but there’s no excuse for anyone to work harder than you do. – Derek Jeter

To uncover your true potential you must first find your own limits and then you have to have the courage to blow past them. – Picabo Street

It is not the size of a man but the size of his heart that matters. – Evander Holyfield

It’s not whether you get knocked down; it’s whether you get up. – Vince Lombardi

Never give up! Failure and rejection are only the first step to succeeding. – Jim Valvano

You can’t put a limit on anything. The more you dream, the farther you get. – Michael Phelps

Champions keep playing until they get it right. – Billie Jean King

I never worry about the problem. I worry about the solution. – Shaquille O’Neal

I’ve learned that something constructive comes from every defeat. – Tom Landry

He who is not courageous enough to take risks will accomplish nothing in life. – Muhammad Ali

You can motivate by fear, and you can motivate by reward. But both those methods are only temporary. The only lasting thing is self motivation. – Homer Rice

Playing Sports Quotes

Hard work beats talent when talent doesn’t work hard. – Tim Notke

Never say never because limits, like fears, are often just an illusion. – Michael Jordan

Without self-discipline, success is impossible, period. – Lou Holtz

Strength does not come from physical capacity. It comes from an indomitable will. – Mahatma Gandhi

I became a good pitcher when I stopped trying to make them miss the ball and started trying to make them hit it. – Sandy Koufax

Do you know what my favorite part of the game is? The opportunity to play. – Mike Singletary

Success is where preparation and opportunity meet. – Bobby Unser

It is more difficult to stay on top than to get there. – Mia Hamm

Age is no barrier. It's a limitation you put on your mind. – Jackie Joyner-Kersee

We may encounter many defeats but we must not be defeated. – Maya Angelou

Sports Lover Quotes

Stubbornness usually is considered a negative; but I think that trait has been a positive for me.- Cal Ripken, Jr.

Do not let what you can not do interfere with what you can do. – John Wooden

It’s not the will to win that matters — everyone has that. It’s the will to prepare to win that matters. – Paul “Bear” Bryant

An athlete cannot run with money in his pockets. He must run with hope in his heart and dreams in his head. – Emil Zatopek

Your talent is God’s gift to you. What you do with it is your gift back to God. – Unknown

Sports Quotes About Winning

The will to win is important, but the will to prepare is vital. – Joe Paterno

It’s not the will to win that matters — everyone has that. It’s the will to prepare to win that matters. – Paul “Bear” Bryant

I’ve failed over and over and over again in my life. And that is why I succeed. – Michael Jordan

A champion is someone who gets up when he can’t. – Jack Dempsey

It isn’t the mountains ahead to climb that wear you out; it’s the pebble in your shoe. – Muhammad Ali

Always make a total effort, even when the odds are against you. – Arnold Palmer

If a team wants to intimidate you physically and you let them, they’ve won. – Mia Hamm

The difference between the impossible and the possible lies in a person's determination. – Tommy Lasorda

Sports Quotes About Confidence

Run when you can, walk if you have to, crawl if you must; just never give up. – Dean Karnazes

Winning isn’t everything, but wanting to win is. – Vince Lombardi

When you’ve got something to prove, there’s nothing greater than a challenge. – Terry Bradshaw

If you can believe it, the mind can achieve it. – Ronnie Lott

Sports do not build character. They reveal it. – Heywood Broun

You have to be at your strongest when you’re feeling at your weakest. – Unknown

Sports Quotes Failure

I’ve failed over and over again in my life. And that is why I succeed. – Michael Jordan

If you fail to prepare, you’re prepared to fail. – Mark Spitz

You miss 100 percent of the shots you don’t take. – Wayne Gretzky

Winners never quit and quitters never win. – Vince Lombardi

What makes something special is not just what you have to gain, but what you feel there is to lose. – Andre Agassi

If you are afraid of failure you don’t deserve to be successful! – Charles Barkley

Persistence can change failure into extraordinary achievement. – Matt Biondi

Sports Quotes Short

It never gets easier. You just get better. – Jordan Hoechlin

It ain’t over ’til it’s over. – Yogi Berra

You’re never a loser until you quit trying. – Mike Ditka

Success trains. Failure complains. – Unknown
